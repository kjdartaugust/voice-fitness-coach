# Recording script

Record each line as one clean take, ~0.5s silence before/after. Speak with the energy
of a coach running alongside the athlete. Keep numbers crisp — they are reused.
Filename the take with the segment id, or mark boundaries in `<dialect>.marks.json`.

Columns: `segment_id` — English meaning — suggested wording (native speaker may adjust).

## Cues
```
speed_up        "speed up a little"       twi: Yɛ ntɛm kakra   ga: Fee oya          ewe: Ɖe abla
slow_down       "ease off / slow down"    twi: Brɛ wo ho ase   ga: Ba shi blɛoo     ewe: Ɖe blewuu
on_pace         "you're on pace, good"    twi: Wo so bɔ yie    ga: Oyɛ jogbaŋŋ      ewe: Èle edzi nyuie
cadence_low     "quicker steps"           twi: Bɔ wo nan ntɛm  ga: Kã onaji anɔkɔ   ewe: Zɔ kaba wu
overstriding    "shorter steps"           twi: Yɛ wo anammɔn tia  ga: Fee onaji bibioo  ewe: Wɔ afɔɖeɖe kpui
halfway         "halfway there"           twi: Woaduru mfimfini   ga: Oshɛ teŋgbɛ    ewe: Èɖo titina
finish          "you finished, well done" twi: Woawie! Mo ne wo   ga: Ogbe naa! Ayekoo  ewe: Èwu enu! Ayikoo
encourage       "keep going, you can"     twi: Kɔ so! Wobɛtumi    ga: Ya nɔ! Obaanya  ewe: Yi edzi! Àte ŋu
interval_work   "go — start the interval" twi: Fi ase — tu mmirika  ga: Bɔ shishii — jo foi  ewe: Dze egɔme — ƒu du
interval_rest   "recover / easy now"      twi: Home kakra      ga: Hejɔɔ fioo       ewe: Ɖe gbɔ vie
split_done      "...done" (after km num)  twi: awie            ga: egbe             ewe: wu enu
remaining       "...remaining" (after m)  twi: aka             ga: eshwɛ            ewe: susɔ
```

## Units
```
unit_m          "metres"                  twi: mita            ga: mita             ewe: mita
unit_km         "kilometre(s)"            twi: kilomita        ga: kilomita         ewe: kilomita
```
